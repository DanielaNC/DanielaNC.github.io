    2022

    What is it about?

    SpAI Tag is an AI system simulation. It consists of three groups of agents: Police, Citizens, and Criminals. The objective of the simulation is to compare the results of different behaviours being applied to the Police agents.

    Development

    With this, the objective was to simulate a real-life scenario with a complex system of agents. We settled for a classical game of Spy Tag with Police and Criminals. We added Citizens to be robbed, as there would be no motive to arrest the Criminals. The Citizen agents have a very simple AI behaviour, they simply wander around and scream when they notice they have been robbed.

    We designed three different behaviours for the Police agents: Reactive, Deliberative, and Cooperative. With Reactive, the Police only acts when something happens, i.e., a Citizen screams. In Deliberative, the Police agents keep a suspect list that they use to pursue and arrest criminals. Finally, in the Cooperative architecture the Police agents elect a leader. This leader then directs the other agents on how to act.

    Because I was the most experienced with Unity and this type of AI, I coded most of the necessary scripts and later added audio cues to help identify the interactions taking place on the simulation.

    So in conclusion ...

    Multi-Agent systems are complex. This project allowed me to explore NPC behaviour with different algorithms and methods, as well as create a simulated real-life event that could be applied to future projects.
